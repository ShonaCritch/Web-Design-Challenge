Humidity


It is apparent that higher humidity percentages are recorded across latitude positions.

The driest cities with less than 60% humidity are mostly clustered within the latitude range of 10 to 40, both in the Northern and Southern hemispheres (+ and -).

Whilst there is a pattern between a cities latitude and humidity (%) shown where the lowest humidty is apparent at corresponding latitudes in each hemisphere, a greater number of cities have humidity between 60% and 100% showing a greater spread of cities with no pattern across latitudes.

It seems likely that drier, more arid regions support lower populations and therefore have less cities.
It was therefore predicted that no statistically significant relationship would be found between the latitude of cities and their humidity (%).
