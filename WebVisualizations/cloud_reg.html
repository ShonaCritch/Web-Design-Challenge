Cloudiness


It is apparent that cloudiness percentages vary across cities, with no obvious realationship between latitudes and cloudiness percentage.

There are however patterns across given percentages, that indicate that many cities share the same clouidness percentage, this may be due to the way that percentages are calculated and rounded.

It was therefore predicted that no statistically significant relationship would be found to exist between the latitude of cities and their cloudiness. For more please see the latitude vs. weather regressions analysis for the hemipshere of interest.
